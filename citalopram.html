Is Citalopram Safe During Pregnancy?

  • 1st Trimester: Use with caution (possible risk of heart defects)
  • 2nd Trimester: Generally considered safe
  • 3rd Trimester: May cause neonatal withdrawal symptoms

Is Citalopram Safe While Breastfeeding?

Yes, with monitoring. Small amounts of citalopram can pass into breast milk. Infants should be monitored for drowsiness or poor feeding.

Why Might Citalopram Be Used During Pregnancy?

Citalopram is an SSRI antidepressant used to treat depression and anxiety. It may be continued during pregnancy when the benefits to maternal mental health outweigh the potential risks.

What Should I Ask My Provider?

  • Is citalopram the best SSRI for me during pregnancy?
  • Are there any monitoring steps needed for my baby?
  • Will I need to change the dose while pregnant or postpartum?
