Pregnancy Safety

  • 1st Trimester: Use with caution
  • 2nd Trimester: Likely safe
  • 3rd Trimester: Likely safe

Breastfeeding

Likely safe – limited studies show minimal transfer to breast milk

Patient-Friendly Summary

Ondansetron (Zofran) is used to treat nausea and vomiting during pregnancy. Studies suggest a possible small increase in risk during the first trimester, but many providers still use it when the benefits outweigh the risks. Always consult your doctor before use.
