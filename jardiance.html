Jardiance (empagliflozin)

Used to treat type 2 diabetes and reduce cardiovascular risk

Is Jardiance Safe During Pregnancy?

  • 1st Trimester: Not recommended
  • 2nd Trimester: Not recommended
  • 3rd Trimester: Avoid use

Is Jardiance Safe While Breastfeeding?

No. Jardiance is not recommended while breastfeeding due to a lack of safety data and potential for serious side effects in infants.

Why Might Jardiance Be Used During Pregnancy?

Jardiance is used to manage blood sugar and reduce cardiovascular risk in people with type 2 diabetes. However, due to potential risks to fetal kidney development, its use is discouraged during pregnancy.
