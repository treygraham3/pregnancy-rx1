Is Insulin Safe While Breastfeeding?

Yes. Insulin does not transfer into breast milk and is considered completely safe while breastfeeding.

Why Might Insulin Be Used During Pregnancy?

Insulin is used to manage high blood sugar in pregnant individuals with type 1, type 2, or gestational diabetes. Proper blood sugar control helps prevent complications such as large birth weight, preterm delivery, and preeclampsia.
