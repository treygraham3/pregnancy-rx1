Is Albuterol Safe During Pregnancy?

  • 1st Trimester: Safe
  • 2nd Trimester: Safe
  • 3rd Trimester: Safe

Is Albuterol Safe While Breastfeeding?

Yes. Albuterol is considered safe for breastfeeding and does not significantly enter breast milk.

Why Might Albuterol Be Used During Pregnancy?

Albuterol is a fast-acting inhaler used to manage asthma symptoms. Maintaining good asthma control is essential during pregnancy to ensure oxygen delivery to the baby.

What Should I Ask My Provider?

  • Can I continue using my rescue inhaler during pregnancy?
  • Should I adjust the dose or frequency?
  • Are there long-term asthma management options that are pregnancy-safe?
