Is Bupropion Safe During Pregnancy?

  • 1st Trimester: Use with caution (possible risk of heart defects)
  • 2nd Trimester: Generally considered safe
  • 3rd Trimester: Generally considered safe

Is Bupropion Safe While Breastfeeding?

Use with caution. Bupropion passes into breast milk in small amounts. Monitor for side effects like irritability or poor feeding.

Why Might Bupropion Be Used During Pregnancy?

Bupropion is often used for patients who have not tolerated SSRIs. It may also be prescribed to help with smoking cessation during pregnancy, which can greatly improve outcomes.

What Should I Ask My Provider?

  • Is bupropion appropriate if I’ve tried other antidepressants?
  • Can I use this to help quit smoking while pregnant?
  • Will my baby need to be monitored if I breastfeed on this medication?
