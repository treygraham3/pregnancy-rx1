Fluoxetine

Used to treat depression, anxiety, and obsessive-compulsive disorder

Is Fluoxetine Safe During Pregnancy?

  • 1st Trimester: Use with caution (some risk reported)
  • 2nd Trimester: Generally considered safe
  • 3rd Trimester: May cause withdrawal symptoms in newborns

Is Fluoxetine Safe While Breastfeeding?

Yes, with monitoring. Fluoxetine passes into breast milk in small amounts. Infants should be monitored for side effects such as irritability or poor feeding.

Why Might Fluoxetine Be Used During Pregnancy?

Fluoxetine is an SSRI antidepressant. Managing depression and anxiety during pregnancy is critical to protect maternal well-being and ensure good prenatal care. The risks of untreated mental health conditions often outweigh the small risks associated with medication.

What Should I Ask My Provider?

  • Is fluoxetine the right SSRI for me during pregnancy?
  • Will I need to adjust my dose during or after pregnancy?
  • What should I watch for in my baby if I continue this medication?
