Is Levothyroxine Safe While Breastfeeding?

Yes. Levothyroxine is considered safe while breastfeeding and does not affect milk production or infant thyroid levels when used appropriately.

Why Might Levothyroxine Be Used During Pregnancy?

Levothyroxine is used to treat hypothyroidism, a condition where the thyroid gland does not produce enough hormones. Adequate thyroid hormone is critical during pregnancy for the development of the baby’s brain and nervous system.
