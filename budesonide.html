Is Budesonide Safe During Pregnancy?

  • 1st Trimester: Safe
  • 2nd Trimester: Safe
  • 3rd Trimester: Safe

Is Budesonide Safe While Breastfeeding?

Yes. Budesonide is considered compatible with breastfeeding and poses minimal risk to infants.

Why Might Budesonide Be Used During Pregnancy?

Budesonide is used as a maintenance inhaler for individuals with asthma. Preventing flare-ups and maintaining stable lung function is important for both maternal and fetal oxygenation.

What Should I Ask My Provider?

  • Is budesonide the best maintenance option for my asthma?
  • Can I use it alongside my rescue inhaler?
  • Will my asthma treatment plan change after delivery?
