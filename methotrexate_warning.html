What is Methotrexate used for?

Methotrexate is used to treat autoimmune conditions and certain cancers.

Is Methotrexate safe during pregnancy?

No. Methotrexate is not safe to take during pregnancy. It is toxic to developing embryos and can cause miscarriage or birth defects at any dose.

FDA Pregnancy Risk Explanation

Previously Category X. Should never be used during pregnancy except in cases of ectopic pregnancy under medical supervision.

Safety by Trimester

1st Trimester: Contraindicated. Very high risk of miscarriage or fetal deformity.

2nd Trimester: Avoid use.

3rd Trimester: Never permitted.

What are safer alternatives?

Talk to your doctor about switching to safer medications. Options may include:

  • Hydroxychloroquine
  • Azathioprine

Why is Methotrexate harmful during pregnancy?

Methotrexate blocks folate, which is essential for fetal cell division and development. It is highly toxic to an embryo.

What should I do if I'm taking Methotrexate and find out I'm pregnant?

Stop methotrexate immediately and contact your doctor. Avoid pregnancy for at least 3 months after stopping.
