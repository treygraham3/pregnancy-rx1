Nifedipine

Used to treat high blood pressure and preterm labor

Is Nifedipine Safe During Pregnancy?

  • 1st Trimester: Generally considered safe
  • 2nd Trimester: Generally considered safe
  • 3rd Trimester: Generally considered safe

Is Nifedipine Safe While Breastfeeding?

Yes. Nifedipine passes into breast milk in very small amounts and is considered safe for use while breastfeeding.

Why Might Nifedipine Be Used During Pregnancy?

Nifedipine is a calcium channel blocker used to treat high blood pressure and sometimes to delay preterm labor. It helps relax blood vessels and reduce uterine contractions.

What Should I Ask My Provider?

  • Is nifedipine better for me than labetalol or methyldopa?
  • What side effects should I watch for?
  • Can I continue nifedipine while breastfeeding?
