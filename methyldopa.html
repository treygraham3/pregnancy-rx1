Methyldopa

Used to treat high blood pressure during pregnancy

Is Methyldopa Safe During Pregnancy?

  • 1st Trimester: Considered safe
  • 2nd Trimester: Considered safe
  • 3rd Trimester: Considered safe

Is Methyldopa Safe While Breastfeeding?

Yes. Methyldopa is compatible with breastfeeding and is excreted in very small amounts in breast milk.

Why Might Methyldopa Be Used During Pregnancy?

Methyldopa is one of the oldest and most well-studied blood pressure medications in pregnancy. It is often used to manage chronic hypertension and is associated with minimal risks to the fetus.

What Should I Ask My Provider?

  • Is methyldopa right for my type of blood pressure?
  • How does it compare to nifedipine or labetalol?
  • Will I need to adjust my dose later in pregnancy?
