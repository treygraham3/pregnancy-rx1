🛑 Drugs to Avoid During Pregnancy

This page lists medications that are known to be unsafe or contraindicated during pregnancy. Please consult your healthcare provider before starting or stopping any medication.

🩺 Hypertension

Medications: Lisinopril, Losartan, ACE inhibitors, ARBs

Reason: Risk of fetal kidney damage and birth defects

❤️ Heart Failure / High Blood Pressure

Medications: Spironolactone

Reason: Anti-androgenic effects

🧈 Hyperlipidemia

Medications: Statins (e.g., Atorvastatin)

Reason: Cholesterol needed for fetal development

🧠 Epilepsy / Mood

Medications: Valproic Acid

Reason: High risk of neural tube defects

🧴 Acne

Medications: Isotretinoin (Accutane)

Reason: Extremely teratogenic

🧫 Infections

Medications: Tetracyclines (e.g., Doxycycline)

Reason: Affects bone growth and tooth coloration

💤 Anxiety / Sleep

Medications: Lorazepam, Benzodiazepines

Reason: Withdrawal and sedation in newborn

🌀 Migraines

Medications: Ergotamines

Reason: Can restrict uterine blood flow

🩸 Blood Thinners

Medications: Warfarin

Reason: Fetal bleeding, skeletal defects

🧬 Autoimmune / Cancer

Medications: Methotrexate, Mycophenolate

Reason: Strong teratogens – absolutely contraindicated
