Is Escitalopram Safe During Pregnancy?

  • 1st Trimester: Use with caution (limited data)
  • 2nd Trimester: Generally considered safe
  • 3rd Trimester: May cause neonatal withdrawal symptoms

Is Escitalopram Safe While Breastfeeding?

Yes, with monitoring. Escitalopram levels in breast milk are low. Infants should be observed for any side effects.

Why Might Escitalopram Be Used During Pregnancy?

Escitalopram is a commonly prescribed SSRI. It may be used in pregnancy if the benefit of treating maternal mental health outweighs potential fetal risks.

What Should I Ask My Provider?

  • Is escitalopram preferred over other SSRIs?
  • What are the signs of withdrawal in newborns?
  • Can I continue this medication while breastfeeding?
