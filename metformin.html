Metformin

Used to manage blood sugar in type 2 diabetes and polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS)

Is Metformin Safe During Pregnancy?

  • 1st Trimester: Generally considered safe
  • 2nd Trimester: Generally considered safe
  • 3rd Trimester: Generally considered safe

Is Metformin Safe While Breastfeeding?

Yes. Only low levels of metformin pass into breast milk and it is generally considered safe for breastfeeding mothers.

Why Might Metformin Be Used During Pregnancy?

Metformin is used to treat type 2 diabetes and gestational diabetes. It helps control blood sugar levels and may reduce the need for insulin in some pregnant individuals. It is also used in women with PCOS to support fertility and hormonal balance during pregnancy.

What Should I Ask My Provider?

  • Is metformin the right option for my pregnancy?
  • Should I continue metformin if I become pregnant while taking it?
  • Will I need to switch to insulin later in pregnancy?
